AD Tycoon is an ad watching tycoon game and ad clicker button game where you press a big red button to start ads, grind points, and buy upgrades.

If you like tycoon games, button games, or grinding games where you build numbers forever, this little ad game lets you grow your own advertisement grinder empire with fun ads and rewards.
